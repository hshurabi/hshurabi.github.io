Research

Exploring the frontiers of computational science and data analytics

Research Overview

My research focuses on developing innovative computational methods and data-driven approaches to solve complex problems across multiple domains. I lead a dynamic research group that bridges theoretical computer science with practical applications in various industries.

Current Research Projects

Advanced Machine Learning for Healthcare

Ongoing

Developing novel deep learning architectures for medical image analysis and diagnostic prediction. This project involves collaboration with medical professionals and has shown promising results in early disease detection.

Machine Learning Healthcare Computer Vision
Funding: NIH Grant R01 - $2.5M (2022-2026)

Scalable Data Analytics Platform

Ongoing

Creating a distributed computing framework for processing large-scale datasets in real-time. The platform is designed to handle petabyte-scale data with minimal latency and maximum efficiency.

Big Data Distributed Systems Cloud Computing
Funding: NSF Award - $1.8M (2023-2027)

Quantum-Classical Hybrid Algorithms

Ongoing

Investigating the potential of quantum computing for optimization problems by developing hybrid algorithms that leverage both quantum and classical computing paradigms.

Quantum Computing Optimization Algorithms
Funding: DOE Quantum Initiative - $1.2M (2023-2026)
